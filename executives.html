Executive Body (2024-2025)

The visionary leaders steering NSU Communications Club towards excellence.

Md Saadat Tariq

President

Md Saadat Tariq embodies structured leadership, quiet ambition, and a deep commitment to excellence. Since joining the North South University Communications Club (NSUCC) in 2022, he has grown from an introverted newcomer into a versatile leader with hands-on experience in administration, sponsorship, marketing, graphics, logistics, and strategic coordination. His precision and reliability have consistently strengthened the club’s professional standards.

As President for the 2024–2025 term, Saadat led NSUCC through a period of organisational refinement and impactful growth. He formalized internal structures, streamlined workflows, and established the club’s first Human Resources Department—initiatives that have reshaped the club’s culture toward long-term development and operational maturity. Under his leadership, NSUCC also secured major collaborations with UNDP and the World Bank, significantly enhancing the club’s visibility and credibility.

Saadat’s presidency is defined by clarity, consistency, and a vision-oriented approach, leaving behind a foundation of efficiency, professionalism, and purpose for future leaders to build upon.

Saiba Adnan

Vice-President

Saiba Adnan Choudhury, the Vice President for the 2024–2025 term, has been the club’s reigning queen of graphics and decor — the creative heartbeat behind so many of NSUCC’s most beautiful events. She is always kind, gentle, and so easy to approach.she is someone everyone feels comfortable turning to, whether for help, advice, or simply a warm presence.She is a proud cat mom to Zoro and her sweetness naturally reflects in the way she treats people and in the thoughtful, passionate artistry she brings to her work. Over the years, Saiba has poured her creativity into events after events, from leading our decor team during Recruitment of 2024 to shaping the visuals and graphics for countless events. She was also the OCH during the first Communicado and there she infused every detail with vision and dedication, transforming ideas into experiences that no one will ever forget. Her artistry, her kindness, and her constant willingness to be there for others have left an irreplaceable mark on NSUCC. She will truly be missed, and the club will forever cherish the creative legacy she leaves behind.

Isnat Hossain Rijon

General Secretary

Isnat Hossain Rijon is our ever-dependable General Secretary for 2024-2025 and he joined NSUCC in 2022. He is our undisputed king of logistics. From managing large-scale setups to assisting with admin work like preparing and submitting memos, he has always shown up with unwavering reliability. No matter what the situation is, if you need him, he will always try to be there for everyone & everything. He has a unique ability to solve problems, find solutions, and connect people, which has made him the go-to person for every tricky, unexpected task. He does not just offer his work ethic but also brings friendliness, positivity, and an energy to the club that will be missed everyday.

Annan Rahman Sahil

Treasurer

Annan Rahman Sahil joined NSUCC in 2022, and has been our beloved, ever-reliable Treasurer, taking care of admin work and logistics with dedication and heart. Despite juggling his own family responsibilities, he’s always there whenever the club needs him, quietly making sure everything runs smoothly.

Out of hundreds of memorable examples of his commitment to the club, one of them comes from the first Communicado. When there was no one available to help with poster-hanging, he took it upon himself to hang every single poster alone, all over the campus. A few were slightly misplaced though, and that definitely earned him a scolding—but the appreciation he received afterward made it all worth it to him.

That moment perfectly captures who he is: a hardworking, humble, and relentless team player who never hesitates to get the job done. He will truly be missed, and NSUCC will always cherish his legacy.

Armeen Hossain

Joint Secretary (Internal Affaris)

Armeen Hossain has been a defining presence at NSUCC since joining in 2019. Over the years, she has grown into a true pillar of the club — a leader whose warmth, humour, and bubbly energy make her instantly approachable, yet whose sharp discipline and firm authority command respect when it matters most. She knows exactly how to balance friendliness with firmness, embodying the rare ability to motivate everyone with kindness.

She is a natural leader in our Writing team and has shaped the club’s people management standards, nurturing countless members into becoming better. Her instinct for understanding people, combined with her extensive experience, has made her the unofficial “boss” of the writing at CC.

Her legacy lies not only in her longevity but in the energy she brings to every room, filled with fun and love. She is leaving behind a legacy of growth, confidence, and unapologetically authentic leadership.

Atin Annan Al Haque

Joint Secretary (External Affairs)

Atin is the silent professional of NSUCC, the steady mountain whose presence anchors the club and whose absence is immediately felt. Throughout his tenure, he balanced personal challenges with unwavering dedication, always putting the club first. Once known as the jolly giant and big brother of NSUCC, he was always there with a warm smile, ready to help or simply brighten someone’s day. As the backbone of the Graphics Team, he became everyone’s first teacher, always saying, “no problem, I’ll teach you,” embodying the true spirit of a gentleman. As External Joint Secretary, his charm, diplomacy, and calm leadership helped maintain strong relationships with NSU authorities and other clubs. We could write endlessly about him, but the truth is simple: Atin, your quiet sacrifices have never gone unnoticed, and NSUCC will never be the same without you.
