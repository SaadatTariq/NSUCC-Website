Executive Body (2024-2025)

The visionary leaders steering NSU Communications Club towards excellence.

Md Saadat Tariq

President

Md Saadat Tariq embodies structured leadership, quiet ambition, and a deep commitment to excellence. Since joining the North South University Communications Club (NSUCC) in 2022, he has grown from an introverted newcomer into a versatile leader with hands-on experience in administration, sponsorship, marketing, graphics, logistics, and strategic coordination. His precision and reliability have consistently strengthened the club’s professional standards.

As President for the 2024–2025 term, Saadat led NSUCC through a period of organisational refinement and impactful growth. He formalized internal structures, streamlined workflows, and established the club’s first Human Resources Department—initiatives that have reshaped the club’s culture toward long-term development and operational maturity. Under his leadership, NSUCC also secured major collaborations with UNDP and the World Bank, significantly enhancing the club’s visibility and credibility.

Saadat’s presidency is defined by clarity, consistency, and a vision-oriented approach, leaving behind a foundation of efficiency, professionalism, and purpose for future leaders to build upon.
