About NSUCC

Discover the story, mission, and vision behind North South University's premier communications club.

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, the North South University Communications Club (NSUCC) is a student-run organisation dedicated to fostering confidence, eloquence, and leadership among the next generation of communicators. From public speaking and debate to creative writing, digital communication, event hosting, and corporate engagement, NSUCC provides students with a dynamic platform to refine their voice, express their ideas, and strengthen their presence in both academic and professional settings.

Driven by the vision of bridging the gap between potential and opportunity, NSUCC has grown into one of the most active and versatile clubs at North South University. We organise high-impact programmes such as Hear2Speak, Job Column, and our flagship inter-university business communication case competition, Communicado, which draws participation from universities across the country. These initiatives help students build confidence, sharpen critical thinking, and develop practical communication skills that extend far beyond the classroom.

NSUCC’s influence reaches beyond the university through notable collaborations with global institutions, including the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and the World Bank. These partnerships allow our members to engage in meaningful discussions on communication, development, and global citizenship, further strengthening the club’s reputation and impact.

At its core, NSUCC is a community. Our diverse and dedicated membership brings together individuals who support one another through collaboration, mentorship, and shared purpose. We strive to cultivate an inclusive environment where ideas flow freely, talents are nurtured, and leaders emerge—leaders who carry their skills and confidence into the world beyond NSU.

NSUCC is more than a club. It is a space where voices rise, stories matter, and communicators are shaped for the future.
