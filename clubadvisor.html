About Our Club Advisor

Syed Rafid Uddin, Former Treasurer for the fiscal year 2023–2024 and the current Club Advisor of the NSU Communications Club, brings years of hands-on club experience and deep organisational insight to guide the Executive Board. His expertise in decision-making, conflict resolution, and structural management has been instrumental in shaping a strong and steady leadership culture within NSUCC.
