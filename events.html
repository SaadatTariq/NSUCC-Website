Our Events

Discover the exciting events, workshops, and competitions that shape our communication community.

Current & Upcoming Events

Join us in our journey of communication excellence through engaging events, skill-building workshops, and networking opportunities that connect students with industry professionals and fellow communication enthusiasts.

Job Column

2025
Season 4 - Flagship Event
Title Sponsor: Pubali Bank Ltd.

Job Column is NSUCC's flagship annual career development initiative, designed to bridge the gap between student potential and professional opportunity. Season 4 expanded into a comprehensive career seminar and job fair, featuring distinguished speakers and recruitment partners.

Season 4 Highlights:

  • Theme: "Navigating the Job Market of Bangladesh"
  • Recruitment Partners: Augmedix, UNDP Bangladesh, Yeamazing, Greenbud
  • Distinguished Speakers from UNDP Bangladesh & NSU CPC
  • Full-day Career Seminar + Interactive Job Fair
  • Direct Employment Opportunities for NSU Students

Our flagship public speaking and communication competition designed to empower students to find their voice and express their ideas with confidence. Hear2Speak provides a platform for students to showcase their oratory skills and engage in meaningful dialogue.

  • Skill Development Workshops
  • Networking with Communication Experts

Communicado

2023
Best Communicator Title
Business Case Competition

Communicado is an inter-university business communication case competition where students present innovative ideas to improve brand communication strategies. Winners receive prize money and exciting gifts while competing for the prestigious "Best Communicator" title.

Competition Features:

  • Brand Communication Strategy Presentations
  • Inter-University Competition Format
  • Prize Money & Exciting Gifts for Winners
  • Presented by Energypac Electronics
  • Co-Sponsored by Global Brand Pvt Ltd. & LeatherX

Amplify

2025
Creative Expression Showcase
Season 1 Completed

Amplify is NSUCC's creative communication initiative celebrating the power of voice, storytelling, and authentic self-expression. This platform provides students opportunities to explore public speaking and creative writing in a competitive yet supportive environment.

Season 1 Features:

  • Online Preliminary Screening Process
  • On-Campus Grand Finale Event
  • Original Speeches & Written Pieces
  • Themes: Identity, Resilience & Personal Narrative
  • Structured Judging & Quality Standards

The Vocal Project (TVP)

2024
Biannual to Triannual Event
Intra-University Competition

The Vocal Project is a biannual to triannual intra-university public speaking competition and student development program. This semi-impromptu competition encourages NSU students to explore and practice their speaking skills in a supportive, low-pressure environment.

Event Format:

  • Random Topic Selection & 2-Minute Speeches
  • 1-2 Minutes Brainstorming Time
  • Judge's Choice & Popular Choice Winners
  • Effective Public Speaking Advisory Sessions
  • Prizes: Books, Certificates & Sponsor Gifts (Kangaroo)

Wordshop

2024
Interactive Workshop
6 Successful Iterations

Wordshop is an annual interactive event that educates its audience on turning creative inspiration into ambition. This workshop-based session provides insights on transforming hobbies or passions into careers, connecting NSU students with industry experts.

Past Workshop Topics:

  • Fiction Writing (NSU EML Faculty)
  • Poetry (The Versemongers)
  • Journalism & Cartooning (Colours Magazine)
  • Teaching (Teach for Bangladesh)
  • Content Creation (Sadman Sadik, Enayet Chowdhury)
  • Startups (Shikho & Pathao Co-founders)

The Final Whistle

2025
Intra-Club Tournament
First Ever Edition

The Final Whistle marked NSUCC's first ever intra-club football tournament, bringing together club members in friendly competition and team building. This inaugural sporting event strengthened bonds within our communication community while promoting recreational activities.

Tournament Features:

  • First Ever NSUCC Football Tournament
  • Intra-Club Competition Format
  • Team Building & Community Bonding
  • Sponsored by ICCB
  • Recreational Sports Initiative
